Special Jharkhandi MaHuWa is a traditional Indian wine that originates from the state of Jharkhand. This exquisite wine is crafted using a unique ingredient called Mahuwa, which is a fragrant tropical flower found abundantly in the region.

Natural TaaDi

Natural Taadi Drink, also known as Palm Wine, is a traditional beverage enjoyed in various parts of India. It is made from the sap of palm trees, typically extracted from the inflorescence of the date palm or other varieties of palm trees.

HaDia Special (Rice Beer)

Hadia, also known as rice beer, is a traditional alcoholic beverage enjoyed in several regions. It is made by fermenting rice or a combination of rice and other grains, resulting in a flavorful and mildly alcoholic drink.
